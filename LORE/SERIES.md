# DRAGON MON — SERIES ARCHIVE

## The History of Creation Through Dragon Eyes

The Dragon Mon universe is organized into chronological series.

Each series represents a stage in the evolution of existence.

A Dragon is not only an individual collectible.

A Dragon is a witness of a specific moment in the history of creation.

Every series expands the mythology while preserving the immutable Dragon identity.
