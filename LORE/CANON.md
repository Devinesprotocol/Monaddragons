# DRAGON MON — CANON

## The Eternal Dragon Archive

Dragon Mon is a living digital mythology built around the Monad ecosystem.

The Dragons are symbolic guardians of creation, knowledge, balance, and infinite potential.

They are not simply digital collectibles.

Each Dragon represents an archetype, a chapter of existence, and a perspective in the history of the universe.

The Dragon Mon universe evolves through chronological series.

Each series represents a stage in the development of reality.

---

# THE DRAGON LAW

Every Dragon Mon shares the same immutable Dragon identity.

The Dragon itself never changes.

Immutable attributes:

- Head shape
- Horn structure
- Face geometry
- Eye shape
- Proportions
- Silhouette
- Symmetry
- Camera angle
- Composition
- Matte material

Only collectible attributes may evolve:

- Color
- Aura
- Eyes
- Halo
- Background energy
- Symbolic traits

Every Dragon is the same eternal entity expressed through different energies.
